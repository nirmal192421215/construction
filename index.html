How We Work

Our Process

A transparent, systematic 8-phase approach that ensures flawless execution from vision to handover.

01

Free Consultation

We meet at your site or our office to understand your vision, requirements, and budget.

02

Design & Planning

Our architects create 2D floor plans, 3D elevations, and interior concepts for your approval.

03

Contract & Approvals

Transparent fixed-price contract signed with detailed BoQ. We handle all government approvals.

04

Foundation & Structure

Soil testing, foundation laying, and structural work with Grade-A materials and quality checks.

05

Superstructure Work

Brick masonry, RCC slabs, roofing, plumbing, and electrical rough-in work completed.

06

Finishing & Interiors

Plastering, flooring, painting, kitchen, wardrobes, and all interior finishing work.

07

Quality Inspection

50-point quality checklist inspection with the client. All snag work completed before handover.

08

Handover & Support

Keys handover with completion certificate, warranty documents, and dedicated aftercare support.

What is the average cost to build a house in Chennai in 2025?
The cost to build a house in Chennai ranges from ₹2,399 to ₹3,199 per sq.ft depending on the package, materials, and design complexity. A standard 1,500 sq.ft house typically costs between ₹28 lakhs to ₹52 lakhs. Use our calculator above for a quick estimate.
How long does it take to build a house?
A typical G+1 house (1,200–2,000 sq.ft) takes 10–14 months to complete. Larger villas (3,000+ sq.ft) take 14–20 months. We provide a detailed project timeline before signing the contract, with milestone-based delivery guaranteed.
What is included in your turnkey construction package?
Our turnkey package covers everything: architectural design, structural work, civil construction, electrical, plumbing, waterproofing, flooring, painting, modular kitchen, wardrobes, bathroom fittings, and all interior finishes. You get a move-in ready home.
Do you handle government approvals and CMDA permits?
Yes, absolutely. We handle all government approvals including CMDA building plan approval, TNEB connection, CMWSSB water connection, and completion certificate. Our in-house legal team ensures full compliance with local building codes.
What warranty do you provide?
We provide a 10-year structural warranty (for Premium & Luxury packages) and a 2-year finishing warranty on all packages. This covers any structural defects, waterproofing issues, and finishing deficiencies at no extra cost to you.
